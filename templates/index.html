Your Hardware, Your Canvas

Pico Message Board is a web-based interface for managing content on your Pimoroni Space Unicorn {% include "icon/arrow-top-right-on-square.html" %} LED hardware. Connect your Stellar, Galactic, or Cosmic Unicorn Board for a convenient interface to create art and messages!

Seamless, Wireless Control

Forget reflashing firmware every time you want to change a pixel. By leveraging the Wi-Fi capabilities of the Raspberry Pi Pico 2 W, your board stays connected to the cloud in real-time. Once paired, your device listens for updates automatically. Simply pick your colors, hit send, and watch the magic happen over the air. It is the ease of a web app met with the tactile glow of hardware.

Design, Animate, and Beam

Use a web-based pixel editor to create messages or masterpieces.

  • Create

    Use browser-based tools to build custom designs with live previews.

  • Animate

    Create multi-frame sprite sheets to bring your art to life.

  • Share

    The real magic is in the connection. Have a friend with a Galactic Unicorn? Add them as a friend and beam a piece of art directly to their device from miles away.
